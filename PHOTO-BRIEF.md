# Photo brief for Brevard stump grinding site

Use real/local photos as soon as possible. Avoid stock-looking images if we want trust and SEO moat.

## Needed images

1. Hero image: stump grinder working on a palm/oak stump in a Brevard-style yard.
2. Before/after: one stump before grinding and same area after grinding.
3. Access photo: machine entering backyard through gate or side yard.
4. Multiple stumps: yard with 2-4 stumps marked or visible.
5. Storm cleanup: stump/debris after tree removal, no dangerous active work shown.
6. Operator/equipment: clean machine photo, ideally no license plate/address visible.
7. Local texture: neutral Brevard yard/palm/oak/sandy soil image.

## Shot rules

- Landscape orientation for hero, 1600x1000 or larger.
- Take a few vertical phone shots for mobile/Google Business-style posts.
- Do not show customer faces, house numbers, license plates, or neighbor addresses unless permission is clear.
- Get at least one photo in Palm Bay if that remains the first city target.

## Filenames

Use descriptive filenames, e.g. `palm-bay-stump-grinding-before.jpg`, `brevard-stump-grinder-backyard-access.jpg`.
