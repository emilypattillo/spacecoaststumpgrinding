Stump grinding FAQ

How much does stump grinding cost in Melbourne, FL?

Cost depends on diameter, access, roots, number of stumps, and cleanup. Most quote requests need photos and measurements before a realistic price can be given.

Is stump grinding different from stump removal?

Grinding removes the visible stump below grade. Full stump removal usually means digging out the stump and roots, which is more disruptive.

Can a stump grinder fit through a backyard gate?

Often, yes. Gate width, slope, steps, landscaping, and obstacles all matter. Include access notes when requesting a quote.
