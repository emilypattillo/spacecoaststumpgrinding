Local stump questions

How many stumps before the per-stump price really drops?

From the second stump on — setup is paid once. The curve keeps improving with count, which is why counting everything on the property (and asking the neighbor) is free money.

We're prepping a cleared lot to build. Grinding or removal?

Building over the spots changes the answer — construction areas often want deeper grinds or true removal with compacted fill. Say what's being built where, and the right mix gets scoped honestly.
