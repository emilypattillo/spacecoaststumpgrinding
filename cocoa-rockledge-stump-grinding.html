Local stump questions

Can you work around tenants?

Yes — grinding is a hours-not-days visit, scheduled with notice through the manager. The machine is loud for a while and then the stump is gone forever; tenants tend to approve.

Our Rockledge yard backs the river — soft ground a problem?

Planned for, not surprised by: ground mats, route choice, and timing around soaked weeks. Riverside lawns get treated like the soft ground they are.
