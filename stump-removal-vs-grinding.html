Stump removal vs stump grinding in Brevard County

Most homeowners asking for “stump removal” actually need stump grinding. Grinding uses a machine to reduce the visible stump below grade so the area can be covered with soil, sod, mulch, or landscaping.

When stump grinding is usually enough

  • The stump is a trip hazard or eyesore.
  • You want to mow, mulch, or replant around the area.
  • The stump is left after recent tree removal.
  • You do not need the entire root system excavated.

When full removal may be different

Full excavation can be more disruptive and may be needed for construction, concrete, plumbing work, or major regrading. Most residential yard requests can start with a grinding quote.
